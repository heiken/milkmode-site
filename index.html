Why MilkMode?

We built MilkMode because we wanted something we could open with one hand at 3am, half asleep, and just tap "go." Something that remembers which side we started with last time. Something that feels encouraging, not like falling behind on data entry. No ads. No accounts. No data leaving your phone. Just a calm, simple timer that works.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you might want to know

Is MilkMode free?

MilkMode offers a free 7-day trial with full access to all features. After the trial, it's just $4.99 — a single one-time purchase with no subscriptions or recurring fees.

Can I track feedings without opening the app?

Yes! MilkMode has a Home Screen widget that lets you start, switch sides, pause, and finish an entire feeding session without ever launching the app. You can also use Live Activities on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island.

Does MilkMode track which breast I fed from last?

Yes. MilkMode remembers which side you started with last time and tracks left and right separately during each session, so you always know which side to start with next.

Does MilkMode collect my data or require an account?

No. MilkMode has no accounts, no ads, and no data leaves your phone. Your feeding data stays completely private on your device.

Can I share my feeding stats with my partner or pediatrician?

Yes. MilkMode generates beautiful, shareable feeding summaries that you can send to your partner, pediatrician, or lactation consultant.

What iPhone and iOS version do I need?

MilkMode requires iOS 17.0 or later. Live Activities and Dynamic Island features require iOS 16.1+, but we recommend iOS 17 for the best experience. MilkMode is available on iPhone only.
