Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking a feeding?

On the Timer tab, tap the L (left) or R (right) button to start timing. Tap again to switch sides, or tap Stop when finished.

How do I delete a feeding session?

Go to the History tab, find the session you want to delete, and swipe left on it. Tap the Delete button to confirm.

Is my data backed up?

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud backup enabled for your device, your app data will be included in your device backup.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Go to Settings in the app, scroll to the Subscription section, and tap "Restore Purchase". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Can I track multiple babies?

Currently MilkMode supports tracking one baby at a time. Multi-baby support may be added in a future update.

How do I change my baby's information?

Tap the gear icon (Settings) on the Timer tab to update your baby's name, photo, or birth date.
