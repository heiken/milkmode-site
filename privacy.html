MilkMode Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

MilkMode ("we", "our", or "the app") is designed to help parents track breastfeeding sessions. We take your privacy seriously.

Data Collection & Storage

All data you enter into MilkMode is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. This includes:

  • Baby's name and birth date
  • Baby's photo (if added)
  • Feeding session records (times, duration, breast used)
  • App preferences and reminder settings

Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it (e.g., using the Share Stats feature).

Third-Party Services

MilkMode uses Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. Apple may collect transaction data according to their privacy policy. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. The only way your data can be shared is if you manually use the "Share Stats" feature, which creates an image you can share via your device's share sheet.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual feeding sessions within the app (swipe left on any entry)
  • Deleting the app from your device (removes all data)

Children's Privacy

MilkMode is designed for parents to track their baby's feeding. The app does not collect data from children and is not directed at children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
